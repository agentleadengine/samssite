Notion vs Linear

Docs + databases vs issue tracker.

Notion is flexible docs/databases/project mgmt. Linear is opinionated, fast issue tracking for software teams.

At a glance

NotionLinear
PrimaryKnowledge base + DBIssue tracking
PerformanceCan get slowVery fast
Best forWikis, mixed use casesEngineering teams

When to pick Notion

Wiki, mixed-use, cross-team docs.

When to pick Linear

Engineering issue tracking.

My verdict

Use both: Linear for engineering work, Notion for docs.
