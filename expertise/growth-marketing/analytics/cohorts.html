Cohort dashboards

📖 3 min readUpdated 2026-04-19

Cohort dashboards show how groups of users behave over time. Trend analysis, not snapshots.

Key views

  • Retention by cohort
  • Revenue per user by cohort
  • Feature adoption by cohort
  • LTV cumulative by cohort

What to look for

  • Improvement cohort-over-cohort
  • Anomalies (sudden drop)
  • Segment differences

What to do with this

  • Track cohort retention, not aggregate MAU, the aggregate number hides declining cohort quality for 6-12 months
  • Compare cohort curves across months, if newer cohorts retain worse, investigate product or acquisition channel drift
  • Segment cohorts by acquisition source, organic cohorts often retain 2-3x better than paid, budget decisions follow
  • Review cohort analysis weekly, not quarterly, cohort drift is a leading indicator that becomes terminal if ignored
  • Build cohort charts into your standing dashboard, if cohort analysis requires a SQL query nobody runs, nobody will see the drift
