Segmentation

📖 3 min readUpdated 2026-04-19

Segmentation splits your list by attributes or behavior so you send different content to different people. Relevance drives engagement.

Common segments

  • By signup source
  • By engagement level
  • By past purchase
  • By geographic
  • By product category interest

Behavioral segments

Users who clicked X topic, bought Y product, visited Z page, more relevant than demographic segments.

Start simple

3-5 segments is enough. Don't build 20 and get lost in management.

What to do with this

  • Limit segments to 5-7 meaningful ones, 20+ segments sound sophisticated but fragment your content capacity
  • Segment on behavior over demographics, "clicked last 30 days" beats "female, age 25-34" for email performance
  • Build segments you can actually act on differently, if two segments get the same email, merge them
  • Review segment performance monthly, retire segments that don't show meaningful lift over the baseline broadcast
  • Scale segments gradually, start with 2-3 and add only when content capacity supports it
