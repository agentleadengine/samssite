Spam triggers

📖 3 min readUpdated 2026-04-19

Spam filters look for patterns in content, structure, and sending behavior. Avoiding triggers improves inbox placement.

Content triggers

  • ALL CAPS
  • Excessive exclamation!!!
  • Words: FREE, ACT NOW, GUARANTEED
  • Too many links
  • Large images with little text

Structure triggers

  • Image-only email
  • Shortened URLs
  • Weird formatting
  • Missing plain-text version

Sending triggers

  • Volume spikes
  • Sending too fast to unengaged
  • Purchased lists

Test before sending

Send to Mail-tester.com, get score. Aim for 9+/10.

What to do with this

  • Avoid classic spam phrases especially in subject lines, "free", "guarantee", "act now", "limited time" all trip filters
  • Keep image-to-text ratio balanced, image-only emails pattern-match to phishing and hit filters
  • Never attach files to marketing email, attachments trigger virus scans and signal non-legitimate sending
  • Use your own tracked domain for links instead of bit.ly, URL shorteners on mass sends look suspicious to filters
  • Pre-check every campaign at mail-tester.com, 9+/10 score before sending is the minimum bar
